About Mango Media

Creative production, model workflows, and compute — connected.

Mango Media is a creative AI production and infrastructure company that connects video production, open-source model workflows, and compute resources in one practical system.

We work with brands, creators, agencies, and product teams to move from an idea, campaign brief, model experiment, or compute requirement to production-ready media output.

A project does not have to begin at the same point every time. It may start with a script, a campaign brief, a visual reference, a model experiment, a compute workload, or an infrastructure requirement. Mango Media connects the right capabilities around the real project objective.

Business focus

Mango Media brings creative direction, model-assisted iteration, and hardware-backed processing into one connected workflow. Production, Model Lab, and Compute can each support a focused need, or work together across a complete media production path.

Production, Model Lab, and Compute

Production supports creative direction and production for advertising videos, short films, music videos, brand content, and campaign assets. Model Lab supports open-source model research and adaptation inside the creative process. Compute supports inference, rendering, testing, media processing, and production-scale AI workloads.

Business focus

Mango Media focuses on practical creative AI workflows: production planning, model-assisted exploration, and compute-backed execution. Each capability can support a focused need, or combine into a complete media production path.

Regional focus

Mango Media pays close attention to opportunities across Southeast Asia, including Thailand, Indonesia, and Malaysia. The company is interested in projects that bring together creative production, AI compute, IDC operations, hardware deployment, and regional digital infrastructure development.

How we work

Every engagement starts with the real project objective: the story to tell, the media to produce, the model workflow to test, or the compute workload to run. Mango Media then defines the right combination of production direction, open-source model support, compute resources, and operating partners.
