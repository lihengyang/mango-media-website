Workflow control

Model Lab connects creative intent with model-assisted iteration.

The goal is not only to generate images or videos. The goal is to create a practical review process that helps teams decide what is ready to move into production.

Brief intake Campaign goal, product message, visual reference, target format, and audience.
Reference system Moodboards, style frames, visual examples, prompts, and asset directions.
Review criteria Brand fit, visual consistency, production usefulness, and creative clarity.
Handoff package Selected concepts, prompts, references, briefs, and production notes.
